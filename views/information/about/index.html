The Wave-Gotik-Treffen 2016 will be from
13th May 2016 until 16th May 2016 in Leipzig/Germany.

Wave gothic treffen is a festival with several flavours. There are many different events happening. These can be found under the program tab. We offern theater, several differents bands playing, food, movies, lectures and much more. The festival is located in the beautiful city of Leipzeig located 70 min via train from Berlin.
